About The Event

Explore the joy of meeting new furry friends at our Dog Friends Meetup. It’s a fantastic opportunity for your dog to socialize, play, and interact with other dogs while you meet fellow dog lovers in a friendly, relaxed environment.

Meetup Activities

Our meetups feature a variety of activities designed to encourage interaction and fun for both dogs and owners.

  • Group Dog Walks: A guided walk through scenic routes that are safe and enjoyable for all breeds.
  • Play Dates: Scheduled play sessions in enclosed areas where dogs can play off-leash under supervision.
  • Training Sessions: Group training sessions focusing on basic commands and social behavior, led by professional trainers.
  • Photo Sessions: Capture the moment with professional photo sessions for you and your dog.
